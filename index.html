What services does Khokana IT Hub offer?

We offer website development, mobile app development, UI/UX design, IT training, digital marketing, and tech consultation services for individuals and businesses in Nepal and abroad.

Do you provide internships or training programs?

Yes, we provide both short-term and long-term training and internship programs in web development, graphic design, digital marketing, and more. You can apply online or visit our office.

Can you build custom websites or apps for my business?

Absolutely. We develop custom websites and mobile applications tailored to your business needs using technologies like React, Laravel, Flutter, and more.

How much does a website or app cost?

The cost depends on the project's complexity, features, and timeline. Simple websites start at NPR 15,000 and custom apps start from NPR 50,000+. Contact us for a detailed quote.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Basic websites can be completed in 1–2 weeks, while advanced apps or e-commerce platforms may take 1–3 months depending on features and requirements.
